DUBAI SHIP BUILDING AND ENGINEERING L.L.C

Dubai Shipbuilding and Engineering LLC located in Al Jadaaf area was established in 1997. Since established, it has delivered over 14 hulls while at the same time consistently provided maintenance and repairing services.

With its commitment to provide efficient services to customers, the company strives to develop and maintain a team of highly motivated, committed, determined, effective and efficient personnel within the company.

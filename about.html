About Company

About Company

MUBARAK MARINE LLC is a marine and offshore services organisation with its head office in Dubai, United Arab Emirates and with branch offices in Abu Dhabi, Saudi Arabia and Singapore.The company is certified for ISO 9001:2008, ISO 14001:2004 and OHSAS 18001:2007 standards and provides the following marine and off shore services.

  • Emergency Response and Rescue
  • Offshore Piling & Marine Construction Projects
  • Salvage and Wreck Removal
  • Rig Re-location and Towage Operations
  • Heavy Lift Operations
  • Anchor Handling and Mooring Operations
  • Oil Terminal Operations, Inspection & Support
  • Deep Sea Towing
  • Hook-up Work Accommodation Barges
  • Movement of Project Cargo & Landing Crafts
  • Tanker Berthing and Pull-back Operations
  • Coastal Harbour Towage and Port Berthing Assistance

The company offers a variety of vessels to suit the ever growing needs of its client base within the Middle East and beyond. From its inception, the company’s fleet has grown today to a diverse fleet of 38 vessels. With newbuildings in the pipeline, Mubarak Marine continues to strive to modernise its fleet and cater to the ever changing marine and offshore requirements of the industry.

Our fleet is manned by highly experienced and professional Masters and Crew. Onshore our fleet and crew members are supported by a team of hard working and well trained support staff, who work around the clock to ensure smooth and efficient operation of our fleet. Mubarak Marine is focused on offering its clients with the best marine and offshore services available in the region.

History

  • 1979 Founded by three Emirati brothers as a family run business with its offices based in Dubai, UAE and operating a single vessel with 10 employees.
  • 1982 Within one year of inception Mubarak Marine was able to double its fleet and by 1982 was operating 4 vessels.
  • 1985 Seeing lack of Salvage operators within the region, Mubarak Marine started offering Towage and Salvage services to grounded, sunken and decommissioned vessels. This went on to become one of Mubarak’s specialties and niche service that it offers today.
  • 1988 By the late-80s, the company had ventured into offering pilotage and harbor towage services within Dubai Creek and Hamriyah Port, Dubai. Today it operates the largest fleet of Harbour Tugs in the area and carries out majority of the operations within the Dubai Creek and Hamriyah Port.
  • 1997 Signs a long term 3 year contract with IOOC for Tanker Towing and Berthing Assistance.
  • 1999 Signs a long terms contract with Yemen Hunt oil Company, Yemen for Tanker Berthing and SBM operations.
  • 2002 Accommodation Work Barge “Millennium 1” is delivered, becoming the first vessel of its kind vessel built in the UAE.
  • 2004 Establishes its first overseas office in Singapore.
  • 2005 Signs a 5 year contract with Chevron, Thailand for provision of Work Barge Services at its Benchman Field, Offshore Thailand
  • 2003-
    2006     Working with Nakheel towards clearance of wrecks from sea bed, offshore Dubai and transporting rocks for Palm Jumeirah and World Islands.
  • 2007 Signs a long term contract with the Federal Coast Guard for provision of Patrol Tugs
  • 2008 Establishes its second overseas office in Dammam, Saudi Arabia.
  • 2009 Establishes a branch office in Abu Dhabi.
  • 2010 Takes Delivery of Accommodation Work Barge “Millennium 2” and Anchor Handling Tug “Mubarak Challenger”, both built locally by its sister concern Dubai Shipbuilding & Engineering (DSBE) LLC.
  • 2012 Ground breaking ceremony for securing 33,000sqm space in Dubai Maritime City, a world-class center for maritime activities in the Middle East
  • 2013 The Overall Management Committee of International Marine Contractors Association(IMCA) approves induction of Mubarak Marine in its Middle East & India Section with participation in the Marine Division as a world-wide Contractor Member.
